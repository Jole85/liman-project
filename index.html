Visit Liman

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Novi Sad!

Top activities to do at Liman

Liman Park offers a variety of activities, including relaxing strolls on the central promenade, enjoying the playgrounds, and letting dogs run in the designated space. The park is also a great place for picnics, sports, and simply enjoying nature!

The Štrand offers a variety of activities, including sunbathing, and sports like volleyball and basketball. It also features playgrounds for children and various cafes and bars. In the evenings, the Štrand often hosts concerts and other cultural events!

Karađorđe Stadium is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of Vojvodina. The stadium is one of the most modern stadiums in Serbia and has one of the best pitches in the country. The stadium has a total of 14,853 seats!

The Liberty Bridge, is a cable-stayed bridge over the Danube River. It's a significant landmark, known for its unique design and history. It is a prominent feature of the city skyline and offers great views of the Danube and the Novi Sad!
